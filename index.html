Step 1 — Private Sessions

Each person has a private AI session independently using the starter prompt below. Don't share answers yet.

I want to have an honest therapy session about my relationship and co-parenting situation. Ask me one question at a time about: (1) what feels like it's going well, (2) what I find most frustrating or unfair, (3) what I secretly wish my partner understood about me, (4) what I think I could do better, and (5) what my ideal outcome looks like for our family in the next 6 months. Be compassionate but honest. Give me a clear summary of your observations at the end.
